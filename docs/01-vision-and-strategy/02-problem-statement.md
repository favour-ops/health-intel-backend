# The Problem Statement

## The Hidden Crisis

This is not a "nice to have" app improvement. This is a **national situational awareness crisis**.

---

## ‚ùå What's Broken Today (Nigeria & Most Countries)

### 1. **No Central Visibility into Capacity**

**Reality:**
- Hospital A has 5 available ICU beds
- Hospital B has emergency ward closed
- Hospital C is out of oxygen
- Hospital D is understaffed

**Problem:**
- No one knows this in real time
- A patient in cardiac arrest gets routed to the closed ward
- A child needs oxygen but it's 5km away in a hospital that found out too late

**Cost:**
- Deaths that could have been prevented
- Chaos in emergencies
- Wasted resources

---

### 2. **No Real-Time Reporting of Emergencies**

**Reality:**
- Traffic accident with 10 injuries happens
- Nearest hospital might be full
- Second hospital doesn't know there's surge coming
- Third hospital is 20km away but available

**Problem:**
- No coordination
- Ambulances make decisions with incomplete info
- Hospitals are always reacting, never prepared

**Cost:**
- Preventable deaths
- Hospital overcrowding
- Poor outcomes

---

### 3. **Citizens Don't Know Where to Go**

Citizens face this dilemma in emergencies:

- "Which hospital can help me right now?"
- "Is this serious enough for the expensive private hospital?"
- "Will they have beds?"
- "Do they have the equipment I need?"
- They call friends, waste precious minutes
- They guess wrong, lose more time

**Cost:**
- Every minute of delay increases mortality risk
- Wrong hospital choice = wrong treatment
- Poor outcomes

---

### 4. **Government Reacts Late, Not Early**

**Reality:**
- Disease outbreak happens
- Hospital reports after days
- Pattern goes unnoticed
- Government realizes months later
- Outbreak has spread to 3 states

**Problem:**
- No early warning system
- No pattern detection
- Reactive, not preventive

**Cost:**
- Public health crisis instead of management
- Unnecessary deaths
- Economic impact

---

### 5. **Hospitals Operate in Data Isolation**

**Reality:**
- Hospital A doesn't know Hospital B is full
- No coordination between hospitals
- Competition prevents data sharing
- Each hospital optimizes for itself, not the system

**Problem:**
- Fragmented healthcare
- No system-wide intelligence
- No shared planning

**Cost:**
- Inefficiency
- Uneven resource distribution
- Poor patient outcomes

---

### 6. **Policy Decisions Are Made from Outdated Data**

**Reality:**
- Government makes funding decisions based on last year's data
- Actual problems have changed
- Resources go to the wrong places
- Wrong policies persist

**Problem:**
- Blind policy making
- Resource mismatch
- Ineffective interventions

**Cost:**
- Wasted public funds
- Ineffective policies
- Preventable health crises
